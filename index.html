What is Hult Prize?

The Hult Prize is a global movement empowering young people to create social enterprises that address the world’s most pressing challenges. Known as the "Nobel Prize for Students," the Hult Prize inspires thousands of students worldwide to develop innovative solutions for a better tomorrow. Its mission is to build a generation of leaders who transform the world into a sustainable and equitable place.

Our Sponsors

Iqra Institute Of IT

Shahbaz Ali Mughal is a dynamic and dedicated young principal at Iqra Institute of Information Technology, where he leads with a strong vision to provide quality education in the field of Information Technology. With a focus on empowering students through technical skills, he runs an IT Center that specializes in offering a range of computer courses. His main areas of expertise include Diploma in IT, Graphic Designing, Web Designing, and Basic Programming courses. In addition to his work at Iqra Institute, Shahbaz has also made a significant impact in the broader educational community by sponsoring the prestigious Hult Prize OnCampus Program at Mehran UET SZAB Campus in Khairpur Mir’s. Through this initiative, he has further demonstrated his commitment to fostering innovation and entrepreneurship among students, encouraging them to tackle global challenges and create positive change.

Indus Cyberforce

The mission of Indus Cyberforce is to empower individuals and organizations with cutting-edge knowledge and practical expertise in cybersecurity, quantum computing, and AI-driven technologies. We aim to foster innovation, enhance digital resilience, and create a secure and sustainable future by providing top-notch training, research, and solutions tailored to evolving global challenges. Our focus is on bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world applications, nurturing talent, and contributing to the digital transformation of Pakistan and beyond. As part of our commitment to nurturing the next generation of innovators and leaders, Indus Cyberforce is proud to sponsor the Hult Prize at Mehran University of Engineering and Technology (MUET) SZAB Campus. This initiative reflects our dedication to empowering students and young entrepreneurs to develop innovative solutions to pressing global challenges.
